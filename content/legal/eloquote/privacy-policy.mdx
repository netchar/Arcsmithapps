## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Eloquote ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Quotes you create and save within the app
- Widget configuration preferences
- Notification preferences

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device type and operating system version
- App usage analytics (anonymized)
- Crash reports and performance data

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and improve the app experience
- Deliver personalized quote recommendations
- Send scheduled notifications based on your preferences
- Fix bugs and improve app performance

## Data Storage

All your quotes and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal data on external servers.

## Third-Party Services

Eloquote uses the following third-party services:
- **RevenueCat** — for managing in-app purchases and subscriptions
- **Analytics** — anonymized usage data to improve the app

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete your data at any time by clearing app data or uninstalling the app
- Opt out of analytics in the app settings
- Contact us with any privacy concerns

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
